Learned Tags

<header>
A container for introductory content or a set of navigational links.
<article>
Represents a self-contained composition in a document, such as a blog post or news story.
<abbr>
Defines an abbreviation or an acronym, often using the title attribute to show the full version.
<dl>
The description list tag, used to enclose a list of terms and their descriptions.
<cite>
Used to define the title of a creative work (like a book, a song, or a quote source).
